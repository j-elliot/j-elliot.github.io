"The World is by Design"

The field of design goes far beyond the class I’m writing this for. It goes beyond writing software and fun new computer input/outputs. It goes beyond the creation of new products and services. Planning a marketing campaign involves design and implements design principles. Organizing an office space and mapping the workflow that will be carried out therein involves design and implements design principles. Even lifestyle decisions can be design projects consciously or otherwise. Drive or take the bus? What are the pros and cons? Try both and compare the results...
